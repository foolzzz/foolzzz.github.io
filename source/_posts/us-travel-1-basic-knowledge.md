### 美国的地址

标准地址格式:
地址1: Street Address
地址2: Apt/Suite/Other
城市, 州, 邮编

eg:
- 2445 Augustine Dr, Santa Clara, CA 95054
- 1 Infinite Loop, Cupertino CA 95014
- 1101 S Main St, APT 203, Milpitas CA 95035


### 单位转换

- 重量
1 pound(lb) = 16 ounce(oz) = 0.45 公斤

- 长度
1 inch = 2.54 cm
1 foot = 12 inch
1 yard = 36 inches
1 mile = 1760 yard = 5280 feet

- 体积
1 oz = 29.57mL
1 gallon = 128 oz
1 pint = 2 cups = 473.176mL
